Description
A fiery young man clashes with an unflinching forest officer in a south Indian village where spirituality, fate and folklore rule the lands.
Author
Ruskin Bond is an Indian author. His first novel, The Room on the Roof, was published in 1956, and it received the John Llewellyn Rhys Prize in 1957. Bond has authored more than 500 short stories, essays, and novels which includes 69 books for children.
